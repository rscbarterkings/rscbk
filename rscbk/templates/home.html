Barter is a system of exchange where goods or services are directly exchanged for other goods or services without using a medium of exchange, such as money, but our Portal is little hybreed where we can negotiate the exchange with money too.
BarterKings.in Portal provides the platform to exchange our unused or Idle products/Items with the products with likeminded users.
The concept of BarterKings portal came into existences by the group of IT gurus who have some valued items which are not in use by them anymore and also they can't able to throw away as they are valued. Here the Idea has born to exchange their products with the similar mindset people who wants to exchange their products.
BarterKings.in portal facilitates the user to Add their items/products in the portal with relevant product information and also facilitates them in searching their interested products from the global products which has added by the global users.
Barterkings.in portal is built on the concept of serving the users with no expectation of profits out of the items/products exchange and the portal will not be involving in any financial dealings with the users.
This Portal also provides the information on the Free items available where our users can give away as Freebies*
