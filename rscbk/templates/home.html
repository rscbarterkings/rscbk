BarterKings.in Portal provides the platform to exchange our unused or Idle products/Items with the products with likeminded users.
The concept of BarterKings portal came into existences by the group of IT software architects who have some valued items which are not in use by them anymore and also they can't able to throw away as they are valued. Here the Idea has born to exchange their products with the similar mindset people who wants to exchange their products.
BarterKings.in portal facilitates the user to Add their items/products in the portal with relevant product information and facilitates them in searching their interested products from the products which was added by the global users.
Barterkings.in portal is built on the concept of serving its users with no expectation of profits out of the exchange of items/products and the portal will not be involving in any financial dealings.
